# Action oriented

<aside>

**What does “action oriented” copy means?**

We don’t just inform, we drive action. It’s not just verbs and CTA: our copy serves a purpose. We guide the users towards a series of desired actions by making the copy lively.

</aside>

## 🔑 5 fundamentals to bring life into copy

> 💡 **We translate financial language into something anyone can understand.**
**Why?** Terms used in finance can sometimes be confusing.
> 

> 🪡 **We use direct and efficient language.**
**Why?** We’re here to keep you moving.
> 

> 💼 **We’re friendly and approachable, but keep it professional.**
**Why?** Users entrust their businesses and money to us: it’s sensitive.
> 

> 🤸 **We are uplifting, but we’re not a superficial cheerleader.**
**Why?** Celebrating meaningful success avoid infantilisation.
> 

> 💫 **We know what it means to “restore the joy” in our writing.**
**Why?** Admin is tedious work that often, isn’t our users’ main job.
> 

## 💡 Translating **financial language**

We know the terms used in finance can sometimes be confusing, so we do what we can to explain even the most complicated concepts in simple language.

**This means:**

- We don’t like jargon and use straightforward phrasing whenever possible
- We always explain technical terms or acronyms the first time we use them
- We use relatable, real-world examples to explain complicated concepts

## 🪡 Using direct and efficient language

While our vocabulary is simple and conversational, we prefer to get to the point. We’re not cold or dismissive, we just keep things short (concise) and sweet (empathetic).

**This means:**

- We avoid long-winded copy
- We use everyday language and emphasise easy-to-understand explanations
- We’re honest and aren’t afraid to be direct, but we always take context into account

## 💼 Keeping it professional

We deal with people’s businesses and their money, which they take seriously. So whether it’s a digital ad, job listing, or onboarding email, our writing matches that seriousness with respectful, professional language.

**This means:**

- We don’t use slang or internet speak
- We use proper financial terminology, with explanations
- We don’t use overly familiar language (sorry, dude.)

## 🤸 Uplifting

Encouraging a glass-half-full mindset goes a long way when it comes to building a successful business. So while we’re quick to celebrate meaningful success, we don’t trivialise real achievement by celebrating every time a user clicks a button.

**This means:**

- We provide honest encouragement based on real-world outcomes
- We do our best to create confidence, not false expectations
- We love that you’re reading this!!! It’s so great! But at Shine we don’t go over the top with enthusiasm or exclamation marks…

## 💫 Restoring the joy

The idea of restoring the joy of working for yourself is a fundamental part of our brand mission, which means it should be communicated in our writing too.

**This means:**

- We express Shine’s overall user benefit in terms of additional time, freedom, and/or energy for the user
- We reiterate that the most important benefit of greater efficiency is more time to pursue your passion and build your business
- We celebrate the courage and entrepreneurial spirit of small business owners

Ultimately, we understand that the most meaningful sense of joy and fulfillment comes from successfully running a business, not reading our writing.
