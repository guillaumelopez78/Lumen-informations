## Go further

### **Accessibility**

Screen readers announce emojis by their Unicode description — "grinning face with smiling eyes" — interrupting the reading flow. Descriptions also vary between platforms, leading to inconsistent experiences.

### **Interpretation**

Emojis carry different meanings across cultures and generations. 👍 is considered offensive in some regions. In transactional or error contexts, ambiguity is never acceptable.

### **Visual consistency**

Emojis render differently across iOS, Android, Windows, and web. Older devices may show unsupported characters (□) or outdated designs that don't reflect our visual identity.

### **Tone**

Our product operates in professional financial contexts. Emojis undermine the credibility required in moments that matter — confirmations, errors, legal notices.
