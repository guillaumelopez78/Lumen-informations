## :headset: Customer Support: 3 voices attributes

> **Lead with understanding and validation**
Put yourself in the users’ shoes and valid their feelings.
> 

> **Be encouraging, but steer clear of forced positivity**
Don’t make promises we’re not sure we can keep.
> 

> **Provide clear, actionable solutions or explanation**
Always give a time window.
>

## 📚 FAQ: help the user being independent

> **Simplify**
Ask simple and concise questions in a natural language.
> 

> **Clarify**
Give clear and short answers, stay pragmatic.
> 

> **Instructive**
Organize in logical sections with titles and visuals.
> 

> **Practical**
Write from the user’s perspective, not the product team.
>
