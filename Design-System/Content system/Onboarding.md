# Onboarding

## How to write onboarding steps:

- Use affirmative sentences only: **`Select your main needs`** **`What are you most looking for?`**
- Start your sentence/title with the action verb: **`Define your role in the company`** **`What’s your job?` `Invite people to your team`** **`Team expenses management`**
- Try to add a stepper with the total number of steps to guide users: **`Step 1/3`**

<aside>

## What makes a good onboarding

**Simple and gradual**
Make the user go step by step instead of flood them with information.

**Imagery**
Use icons, illustrations or animation to explain your point.
⚠️ Be wary of ‣.

**Flexible**
Make it possible to skip steps if not mandatory.

**Validation**
Give feedback and validate user’s progress. Be careful not to infantilise.
