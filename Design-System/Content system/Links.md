# Links

## How to insert a link in a text:

- Be specific about where the link goes: `Read our article about how to connect a bank account`
- Underline the destination in priority:  `If the problem persists, contact customer support.`
- If the destination is directly an action, underline the action as well: `You have no invoices yet. Create your first invoice.`
- Avoid vague wordings: `You can read our article about connecting a bank account here.` or `See more`.
