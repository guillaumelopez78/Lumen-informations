# Danish sensitive components

## 🎼 Informal tone vs. Formal tone

- Always use "du" (informal) – formality feels archaic and alienating
- Keep language warm, helpful, and conversational
- Danes value directness and simplicity – avoid corporate jargon
- Avoid overly casual expressions or slang `Din faktura er nu blevet sendt.` `Super! Din faktura er på vej.`

## 🗓️ Numbers and dates

- Months and days of the week use lowercase unless sentence-initial `januar` `mandag` `jan`
- Thousands separator are space or full stop, decimal use a comma `1 250,50` `1,250.0`
- Ordinal format use full stop after the number `1., 2., 3., 4.`
- Phone numbers use country code and spaces to group digits `+45 23 45 67 89`
