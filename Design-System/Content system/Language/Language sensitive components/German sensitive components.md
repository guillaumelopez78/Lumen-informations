# German sensitive components

## 🎼 Informal tone vs. Formal tone

- Default to "Du" (informal) for most SaaS/tech contexts
- Use "Sie" (formal) only for legal documents (such as T&C)
- Don't mix Du and Sie within the same flow
- Even with Du, maintain professionalism and clarity `Deine Daten wurden erfolgreich gespeichert.` `Alles erledigt! Wir haben deine Daten gespeichert!`

## 🗓️ Numbers and dates

- Months and days of the week use use a capital `Januar` `Montag` `Jan`
- Thousands separator are space or full stop, decimal use a comma `1 250,50` `1,250.0`
- Ordinal format use full stop after the number `1., 2., 3., 4.`
- Phone numbers use country code and spaces to group digits `+49 152 234 3333`

## ⚛️ Other sensitive components

- Add a comma before infinitive clauses `Bitte klicken Sie hier, um fortzufahren.`
