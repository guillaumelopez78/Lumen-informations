# Empty states

---

## How to write an empty state message:

#### IF first time empty state

When: the user hasn't created any content yet (invoices, customers, templates, etc.).

- Title structure is: **`No`** + `feature` + **`yet`** as in **`No e-invoice received yet`**
- Body copy should encourage the user to take their first action and explain the value of it: **`Once you receive your first e-invoice, you can manage, download and pay it directly from this page.`**
- Always keep a welcoming, motivating and helpful tone.

#### IF filter or search is empty

When: the user has applied filters or searched, and no results match.

- Title structure is: **`No`** + `search or filter query` as in **`No unpaid invoices`** or **`No clients match your search`**
- Body copy should help the user understand why there are no results and how to adjust their search or filters: **`All your invoices have been paid`** or **`Try adjusting your filters or searching with a different spelling`**
- Always keep the tone solution-oriented.

#### IF upsell or feature-gates screens

When: the user has reached a limit or is viewing a feature available only on a higher plan.

- Title should always state the `main benefit` + `feature name` as in **`Save time with invoice templates`** or **`Invoice easily, collect quickly`**
- Body copy should explain what the feature does before mentioning the limitation: **`Create customised templates for your invoices. This feature is available on Plus and Business plans.`**
- Primary CTA is either **`Upgrade to [plan]`** or **`Discover more`**
- Secondary CTA should redirect to **`See pricing details`**

#### IF loading or temporary screens

When: a background action/process is in progress or a flow is awaiting customer action.

- Title structure is: action verb in -ing form as in **`Syncing your transactions`** **`Generating your report`** or **`Verifying your information`**
- Body copy should explain when content will appear / when modal will disappear and what (if anything) the user needs to do: **`We're connecting your bank account. Your transactions will appear here shortly.` , `We're compiling your financial data. This usually takes a few minutes.`** or **`Your documents are being processed. This should take up to 5 min max.`**
- The tone must be reassuring, patient.
    
    <aside>
    💡
    
    **Best practices:**
    
    - Be specific about timing: "shortly", "within a few minutes"
    - Reassure that everything is working as expected
    - Provide a CTA if the user can take action: "Resend verification email", "Refresh"
