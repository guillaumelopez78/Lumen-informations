### Is Shine Free?

**Can I use Shine for free?**

In one word, yes! Our free plan is a great way to get started. It lets you create and send invoices that you can send via email or download as a PDF. To learn more, check out our plans.

**Are there limits to Shine’s free plan?**

We’ve designed our free plan for freelancers and business owners who are just starting out. It lets you send unlimited invoices per month and add five client profiles to your dashboard, . When your business outgrows the free plan, you can upgrade to the Shine Start or Plus plans to unlock all kinds of different features. To learn more, compare our plans.

**That’s nice, but why does Shine offer a free plan in the first place?**

Because starting a business isn’t easy! We know what it’s like when admin starts to drain the fun and excitement out of working for yourself. So we want to help small business owners start off on the right foot with quick and easy tools to invoice, track expenses, and store receipts. All the essentials to kickstart your business, completely free with Shine.

**What kind of paid plans do you have?**

We offer two different paid plans that are made to scale with you as your business grows. Our Start plan is designed for freelancers and early-stage businesses, while our Plus plan is for growing businesses servicing several clients. Both include features like customisable invoices, smart tools that help find and automatically categorise your expenses, real-time VAT overviews and a lot more. To learn what you get with our paid plans, explore our plans page.
