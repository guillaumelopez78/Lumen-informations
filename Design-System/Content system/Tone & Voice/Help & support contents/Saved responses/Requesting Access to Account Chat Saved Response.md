### Say It Like Shine: A ‘Requesting Access to Account’ Saved Response - written in the brand voice:

<aside>

Hi [FIRST NAME],

I’m happy to help as best I can, but I’ll need access to your account first. As long as that’s OK with you, just follow the instructions below.

If you’re using a desktop or laptop:

- Click on Settings > Profile > Privacy
- Click the checkbox next to Allow Support – it should show a tick
- Click on Save

If you’re using our mobile app:

- Tap More (in the bottom right corner) > Your Company Icon > Settings
- Tap the checkbox next to Allow Support – it should turn green

When you’re done, just let me know and we’ll get back to solving your issue.

Thanks,

[AUTHOR NAME]

Customer Service Specialist at Shine
